About Me

Eons ago, I graduated with an Applied Associates Degree in Information Technology. During my time in school, I dabbled in simple website design. After graduation, though, my focus shifted towards a different computer science field, and I never took the time to nurture my web Development skills. During the summer of 2024, however, I decided it was time to return to my computer science roots. My research led me to Coding Temple, where I began the Software Engineer Bootcamp in October 2024.

Becoming a Software Engineering sparked my interest because, creative by nature, the thought of being able to develop visually-appealing and intuitive experiences for end-users inspires me. Front end development is so much more than just having an eye for design, though. These developers directly influence user interactions, and I look forward to being able to put users first and create beautiful, yet functional websites in the near future.

When I am not studying, I am an outstanding gluten free baker, highly distracted by books, and more than obsessed with Highland cattle.
